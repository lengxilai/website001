Introduction

Our structural engineers combine expertise in a variety of fields, embracing tall buildings, dynamic and seismic design, conventional and geometrically complex structures. They work with the architects as an integral part of the design team from conception to completion. The engineering team can provide a full service, or offer strategic input at key stages in the design process when working with independent consultants. By challenging one another to explore new structural and creative possibilities in this way, we are able to devise innovative design solutions that are economical, beautiful and sustainable.
